Why GraphReFly?

Three shifts in how
you work with information

Modern tooling drowns you in data. GraphReFly inverts the model: describe what matters, and it works for you.

Stop Drowning in Information

Describe what matters in plain language. GraphReFly watches, filters, prioritizes, and alerts you to what actually needs attention. No more tab overload.

Trust AI by Understanding It

Every decision is traceable through causal chains. Structural causality persists across sessions, so you always know why something changed and can audit the reasoning.

Describe It, Don't Code It

Natural language in, graph composition out. Visually review the topology, run it, then tweak it by talking. Programming becomes a conversation.

Living Scenarios

Intelligence that stays on

These are not one-shot queries. They are persistent, evolving graphs that watch and reason over time.

Email Triage

A graph that watches your inbox, classifies urgency, surfaces action items, and explains why each email needs attention. Runs continuously, learns your patterns.

Spending Alerts

Connect bank feeds to a reactive graph. It detects anomalies, tracks budgets, and alerts you before thresholds are crossed. Always watching, never polling.

Knowledge Management

Feed in notes, bookmarks, and conversations. The graph organizes, links, and surfaces connections you missed. Your second brain, reactively maintained.

A Taste

Feels like a spreadsheet.
Works like an engine.

State, derivation, and effects in a few lines. The graph handles propagation, persistence, and explanation.

example.ts 
const count   = state(0);
const doubled = derived([count], (c) => c * 2);

effect([doubled], (d) => console.log(d));

count.down([[DATA, 1]]);  // logs: 2
