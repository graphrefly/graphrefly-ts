Plate II.
A key to the cartography.

A handful of shapes; a handful of signals. The primitives travel together by convention — every chart in this volume uses the same glyphs.

State
A writable cell. The leaf of the graph. Use g.state().
Producer
A push source. Timers, events, async streams. g.producer().
Derived
A pure function of upstream deps. Diamond-safe. g.derived().
Effect
A side-effect sink. The graph touching the world. g.effect().
External
Human-in-the-loop or out-of-graph input. g.promptNode().
